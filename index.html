About Us

Established in 1991, Ningbo Far East Lighting Co.,Ltd. devoted to designing, manufacturing and marketing for high quality lighting products, has been recognized as a famous manufacturer in lighting. As our major product, Waterproof light fitting achieved great business success in the market, as a result, it was listed in the "China Torch Program".

Far East owns the "Ningbo Illumination of Provincia-level High-tech R&D Center", and the "Zhejiang Lighting Product Quality Testing Center" which was established in partnership with Ningbo Product Quality Supervision & Testing Institute, and we were certified by China's Ministry of Science and Technology, with the "High-tech Enterprise". Far East develops over 20 new products every year, and has obtained more than 100 patents including electrical and mechanical structure, illumination design, electrical control, etc. Far East always focuses on innovation and keeps pace with the development of new technology in lighting field, has achieved ISO9001:2015 and ISO14001:2015 certifications.
